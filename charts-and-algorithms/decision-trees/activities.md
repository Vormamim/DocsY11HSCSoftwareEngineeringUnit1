# Activities

1. **Draw the following decision trees:**
   1. Draw a decision tree for making a cup of coffee. It is up to you what to consider, but make it **at least four** levels down. Consider things like type of coffee, milk alternatives, sugar, etc.&#x20;
   2. Draw a decision tree for choosing a show on Netflix (or other streaming service). Make it **at least four** levels down.
2. Look at the following decision tree on whether or not to cook at home or eat Maccas.

```
                 Do you feel like cooking?        
                        /     \
                    Yes         No
                     /            \
            Do you have time?     Do you have a microwavable meal?
             /          \                 /         \
           Yes           No              Yes         No
           /              \             /             \
    Do you have all    Maccas         Cook          Maccas
    the ingredients?  
        /    \
     Yes      No
     /          \
   Cook       Maccas
  


```

a. I feel like cooking, have time but not enough ingredients. What do I do?

b. I do not feel like cooking, but do have a microwaveable meal. What do I do?

c. I feel like cooking, but do not have much time on my hands. What do I do?
